2. DALL-E (OpenAI)

Best for: Quick generation, ChatGPT integration, image editing

Pros

  • Seamlessly integrated with ChatGPT
  • Excellent at understanding natural language prompts
  • Built-in image editing capabilities
  • API access for developers

  • Aesthetic quality below Midjourney
  • Less artistic control (fewer parameters)

  • Content policy restrictions
