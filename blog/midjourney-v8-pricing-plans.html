Detailed Plan Breakdown

Basic Plan ??$10/month ($8/month annual)

The Basic Plan is the entry point for Midjourney. It's ideal for casual users who want to experiment with AI art generation without a major commitment.

  • ~200 image generations per month (3.3 hours of Fast GPU time)
  • Full access to V8 model and all features including --hd mode
  • Web interface at alpha.midjourney.com and Discord access
  • No Relax mode ??once you run out of Fast GPU time, you'll need to wait until next month or buy more
  • All images are public in the Midjourney gallery

Best for: Hobbyists, occasional users, anyone testing the platform before upgrading.

Standard Plan ??$30/month ($24/month annual) ??Recommended

The Standard Plan is the sweet spot for most users. It offers the best balance of cost and value.

  • ~900 image generations per month (15 hours of Fast GPU time)
  • Unlimited Relax mode ??continue generating even after Fast GPU time runs out (slower queue)
  • Full V8 access with all features
  • Effectively unlimited generations when using Relax mode

?? Our Recommendation: The Standard Plan is the best value for most users. Relax mode means you'll never be blocked from creating ??you'll just wait a bit longer in the queue. At $30/month, the cost per image with Relax mode can drop to just $0.01-0.03 per image.

Best for: Regular creators, designers exploring AI art, content creators, anyone who wants to generate without worrying about limits.

Pro Plan ??$60/month ($48/month annual)

The Pro Plan is designed for professional users and businesses who need more Fast GPU time and privacy features.

  • ~1,800 image generations per month (30 hours of Fast GPU time)
  • Unlimited Relax mode
  • Stealth mode ??your images won't appear in the public Midjourney gallery
  • Priority access during peak hours

Best for: Professional designers, agencies, commercial users who need image privacy, high-volume creators.

Mega Plan ??$120/month ($96/month annual)

The Mega Plan is for power users and teams who need maximum throughput.

  • ~3,600 image generations per month (60 hours of Fast GPU time)
  • Everything in Pro, plus double the Fast GPU allocation
  • Ideal for teams or commercial workflows with high output demands

Best for: Studios, agencies, high-volume commercial operations, teams sharing a single subscription.

V8 HD Mode Pricing Impact

One important thing to understand about V8's new --hd mode: it uses approximately 2x the GPU time compared to a standard generation. This means:

  • A standard V8 image takes ~1 minute of GPU time
  • A V8 --hd image takes ~2 minutes of GPU time
  • On the Basic Plan, you'll get approximately ~100 HD images instead of ~200 standard images
  • On the Standard Plan, Relax mode makes this a non-issue for most users

?? Pro Tip: Use standard resolution for initial prompt exploration and drafting. Once you've refined your prompt and found the perfect composition, switch to --hd for the final, high-quality render. This strategy maximizes your Fast GPU time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes. You can change your subscription plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll receive a prorated credit for the unused portion of your current plan. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do unused GPU hours roll over?

No, unused Fast GPU time does not roll over to the next month. This is another reason why Relax mode (available from Standard and above) is so valuable.

Can I buy additional Fast GPU time?

Yes, all plans allow you to purchase additional Fast GPU time at $4 per hour. This is useful if you occasionally need a burst of extra capacity.

Is Midjourney V8 included in all plans?

Yes! V8 is available on all plans, including Basic. You don't need to upgrade to a higher tier to access V8's features.

What payment methods are accepted?

Midjourney accepts major credit cards, debit cards, Google Pay, and Apple Pay. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.
